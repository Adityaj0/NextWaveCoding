About Us

NextWave Coding is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering individuals through coding education and training. Our mission is to bridge the digital divide and create opportunities for everyone, regardless of their background.

What We Do

We offer a variety of coding courses and workshops for all ages and skill levels. From introductory programming classes to advanced web development bootcamps, our curriculum is designed to equip learners with the skills they need to succeed in the digital world.
